Aerial Yoga

Aerial yoga includes the benefits of asana practice combined with the advantage of incorporating a silk hammock prop. In this class students will explore a practice of both elevation and grounding to increase flexibility, strength and improve structural alignment. They will enjoy the ease of inversions in a fun an playful way yet calm the mind with breath and mindful movement.

Suspension Yoga

We use the Gravatonics suspension apparatus for this class; the combination of the sling and the handles make for unlimited possibilities of movement. During this one hour session you will enjoy the combination of yoga and body conditioning

Anti-Gravity Pilates

Anti-Gravity Pilates simultaneously builds strength, flexibility, balance, and endurance. Traditional Pilates mat and reformer exercises are performed with the addition of anti-gravity suspension straps which provide innovative options for building strength while allowing the body to come into optimal alignment. We focus primarily on core strength which is the best training method for getting an edge in all of the other physical activities you love while helping to prevent injuries down the road. This class will help you gain even more confidence in your running, cycling, swimming, climbing, skiing and much more!

Suspended Strength

Using suspension straps, ceiling-anchored tubing and other equipment, this unique body conditioning class is designed to challenge your body and brain for a more functional, more engaged workout and great results. Small class-size combined with Katherine's very personal training style means lots of one-on-one coaching and customized adjustments for your perfect workout and progressions.

Suspended Barre

This is a one hour workout to upbeat fun music designed to strengthen and tone those muscles ballet dancers are known for. This class combines the best of deep stretching, standing Pilates and classical ballet barre. The suspended barre adds an extra element to challenge and entertain.

H.I.I.T.

High Intensity Interval Training is one of the most popular body conditioning formats today - and for good reason. Great fat burn, super cardiovascular training and functional strength development in one dynamic class. Katherine promises you will never be bored by the H.I.I.T. as each class utilizes various interval timing systems as well as creative equipment combinations and challenging body-weight exercises.

Pilates Group Reformer

The Reformer's spring-loaded system works to develop balance and coordination while strengthening and stretching the entire body. The Pilates apparatus approach offers 500+ exercises and modifications that can be performed by beginner to advanced students. Our group reformer classes are a maximum of 4 students with 1 instructor ensuring you will have the attention you need to excel in this session. We offer "beginner class" which we recommend for first-timers so you can learn the basics and be ready for any of our other classes.

Pilates Sculpt

This mat and prop based program is built on the same foundation of developing balance, coordination, strength and stretching for the whole body. Endless excercise variations ensure that no two classes are the same, challenging your body and exciting your mind.
